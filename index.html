creatAIve - AI-Powered Creative Collaboration Platform

creatAIve is an AI-powered platform that aims to revolutionize the creative ecosystem, providing all creators with easier access to unleash their creativity and achieve growth.

Problem Statement

  • Creators often face a lack of innovative ideas and inspiration, hindering the development of groundbreaking content.
  • Aspiring creators encounter high barriers to entry, limiting the growth of a vibrant creative community.
  • Absence of expert feedback and collaboration opportunities impedes skill development and innovation in the creative process.

Our Solutions

  1. Expert Feedback and Collaboration
  2. Lowering Barriers to Entry
  3. Boosting Creativity

Market Opportunity

The overall size of the art/creative industry market is estimated at around $2 trillion, with the addressable market for AI-powered creative tools, collaboration platforms, and creator education/consulting around $50 billion.

Business Model

  • Individual/team subscription model: $10-60/month
  • Enterprise solutions: Project-based pricing ($1,000-10,000)
  • AI-generated content marketplace: 20% commission on content licensing
  • AI education programs: Online courses, seminars, and consulting

Roadmap

  1. Year 1: Launch MVP with core text/image generation and basic collaboration features
  2. Years 2-3: Expand into specialized creative domains and focus on acquiring professional creators and enterprise clients
  3. Years 4-5: Integrate metaverse/XR solutions for virtual world creation
  4. Year 5+: Pursue global expansion and vertical integration
